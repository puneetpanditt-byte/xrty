Our Journey

1967

Foundation

Maitreyi College was founded by the Delhi Administration with 217 students in a modest school building in Lajpat Nagar.

1970

New Campus

The college shifted to its present location in Chanakya Puri with a new building designed by renowned architect J.K. Chowdhury.

1990s

Growth & Expansion

Addition of new courses, expansion of infrastructure, and establishment of various research facilities and laboratories.

2000s

Digital Transformation

Modernization of facilities, introduction of computer courses, and integration of technology in teaching and learning.

2020s

Excellence & Innovation

NAAC A+ accreditation, increased research output, and focus on holistic development of students.

Present

Leading the Way

Today, Maitreyi College stands as a premier institution for women's education with national recognition.
