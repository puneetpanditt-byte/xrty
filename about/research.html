About Centre for Research

Advancing knowledge through innovative research

The Centre for Research at Maitreyi College is dedicated to fostering a culture of scientific inquiry and academic excellence among faculty and students.

Our research centre provides a platform for interdisciplinary research, encouraging collaboration across various departments and with external institutions. We strive to create an environment that nurtures curiosity, innovation, and scholarly pursuit.

Our Objectives
  • Promote quality research activities
  • Facilitate interdisciplinary collaboration
  • Secure research funding and grants
  • Organize seminars and workshops
