Frequently Asked Questions

What are the eligibility criteria for admission?

Candidates must have passed 10+2 examination from a recognized board with minimum aggregate percentage as per Delhi University guidelines. Subject-specific requirements apply for honors courses.

How can I apply for admission?

You can apply online through the Delhi University admission portal or visit our admission office for assistance. Make sure to fill the form correctly and submit all required documents.

What documents are required for admission?

Required documents include: 10th and 12th mark sheets, character certificate, transfer certificate, migration certificate (if applicable), passport size photographs, and category certificates (if applicable).

Is there any reservation policy?

Yes, reservations are available as per government norms for SC/ST/OBC/EWS/PwD categories. Detailed information is available in the college prospectus and university guidelines.

Need Help with Admission?

Our admission support team is here to help you with every step of the admission process. Don't hesitate to reach out!
